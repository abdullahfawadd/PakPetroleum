# PAK Petroleum Information Architecture

## 1. Core Objectives
Design a homepage and site structure that instantly communicates:
- **Scale**: National reach, infrastructure volume.
- **Trust**: Safety records, compliance, years of operation.
- **Operational Depth**: Complexity of logistics, trading, and technology.

## 2. Navigation Tree (Mega-Menu Structure)

*   **Operations**

    *   Sub-links:

        *   Distribution Network (Fleet, logistics)
        *   Storage Infrastructure (Terminals, safety)
        *   Retail & Commercial (Fuel stations, B2B)
*   **Company**
    *   *Intent: Establish authority and history.*
    *   Sub-links:
        *   Our Story / Heritage

*   **Insights**

    *   Sub-links:
        *   Market Analysis

*   **Contact**
    *   *Intent: Drive partnerships and inquiries.*

## 3. Homepage Content Hierarchy

The homepage is designed to guide the user through a narrative of scale and reliability.

1.  **Hero Section**
    *   *Content:* High-impact visual of infrastructure + Vision Statement.
    *   *Goal:* Immediate impression of industrial power.
2.  **National Footprint**
    *   *Content:* Map visualization, city counts, key terminals.
    *   *Goal:* Demonstrate "Scale" physically.
3.  **Core Operations**
    *   *Content:* Triptych of Trading, Storage, Distribution.
    *   *Goal:* Show "Operational Depth".
4.  **Technology & Safety**
    *   *Content:* Real-time monitoring systems, safety stats.
    *   *Goal:* Prove "Trust" through competence.
5.  **Sustainability & Compliance**
    *   *Content:* Environmental commitments, quality badges.
    *   *Goal:* Verify "Responsibility".
6.  **Metrics & Trust Indicators**
    *   *Content:* Rolling ticker of volume moved, safety hours, uptime.
    *   *Goal:* Quantitative proof of reliability.
7.  **Leadership**
    *   *Content:* Executive team highlights.
    *   *Goal:* Humanize the corporate authority.
8.  **CTA (Partnerships)**
    *   *Content:* Direct line for B2B/B2G engagement.
    *   *Goal:* Conversion.
